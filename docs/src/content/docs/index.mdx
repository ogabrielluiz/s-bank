**S-Bank** is a library of **analog-emulation DSP building blocks** — reusable C++ that gives
VCV Rack modules (and other audio software) convincing analog behaviour and sound. It models
*circuits*, not just filter sweeps: vactrol cells, state-space gates, envelopes, oversampling,
and seeded analog drift, ready to compose into your own instruments.

The library is the product. It ships with two **demo modules** that prove it works and show
how — but the point is the blocks underneath them.

## Why it's analog

The library models the underlying circuit, so the analog character is inherent — not an
envelope or EQ bolted on after the fact:

- The **vactrol cell** is the Parker &amp; D'Angelo state-space Buchla-292 model — the
  opto-isolator's slow attack and long, bloomed release fall out of the model itself.
- The **clean gate** is its inverse: zero-bleed when closed, driven by a shaped envelope,
  with a whisper of seeded drift so "clean" never tips into "sterile."

<Aside type="note">
  Everything is native, header-only C++ with **no VCV-Rack-SDK dependency**, so it tests and
  runs headless — drop it into any audio project, not just Rack.
</Aside>
